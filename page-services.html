Frequently Asked Questions

01. What makes Clapout Events unique in the wedding planning industry?

Since 2018, we've distinguished ourselves through our scientific approach to event planning, combining creativity with precision. Our ability to blend traditional elements with modern luxury, coupled with our experience across South India, allows us to create uniquely personalized celebrations. We're known for our innovative themes and commitment to maintaining the perfect balance between cultural richness and contemporary sophistication.

02. Do you handle destination weddings, and what locations do you cover?

Yes, we specialize in destination weddings across South India and North India. Our primary service areas include Kasaragod, Mangalore, Manipal, Dakshina Karnataka, Jaipur, and Cochin. We handle everything from location scouting to guest accommodations, ensuring a seamless experience. Our team manages local vendor relationships, legal requirements, and cultural experiences to create unforgettable destination celebrations.

03. What is included in your Premium Wedding Planning package?

Our Premium Wedding Planning service is comprehensive, covering everything from initial concept to final execution. This includes venue selection, vendor coordination, timeline management, design and décor, guest relations, and on-site coordination. You'll receive round-the-clock support, exclusive access to our elite vendor network, and a dedicated team managing all aspects of your celebration.

04. How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking our services a few months before your wedding date, especially for peak season celebrations or destination weddings. This timeline allows us to secure the best venues and preferred vendors, develop a comprehensive design concept, and ensure every detail is perfectly executed. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines based on availability.

05. How do you handle pre-wedding events and cultural ceremonies?

Our team is well-versed in orchestrating all pre-wedding celebrations, from engagement ceremonies to mehndi celebrations and cocktail soirées. We ensure each event honors your cultural traditions while incorporating your personal style. Our expertise includes managing multiple events simultaneously while maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity.

06. What kind of documentation services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive documentation services including professional photography, cinematic videography, and drone coverage. Our carefully selected team captures both candid and posed moments, providing same-day editing options. We ensure your wedding story is preserved from multiple perspectives, creating a complete collection of timeless memories.

07. How do you manage guest experiences and accommodations?

Our guest relations team handles all aspects of guest management, from digital RSVPs to international travel arrangements. We coordinate accommodations, local transportation, welcome baskets, and cultural experiences for your guests. Our team ensures clear communication and comfortable arrangements for all attendees, making your wedding a memorable experience for everyone involved.
